# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project maintains a professional, inclusive environment. We expect all
participants — contributors, maintainers, and community members — to treat
each other with respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive in code reviews and discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully; give feedback kindly
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **nick@sigilos.io**.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers who do not
follow or enforce this code may face temporary or permanent repercussions.
